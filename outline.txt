  - company can create new listing (post /listings)
  - company should not be able to edit listing once created
  - company can get info on all listings (get /listings)
  - company can get info on single listing + investors (get /listings/:id)
  - company decides when to close investment (post /listings/:id)
  - company can approve or reject a customer (post /listings/:id)
